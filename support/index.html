Espresso Pro Support

Help and troubleshooting for Espresso Pro on macOS.

Common Checks

  • If Espresso Pro is not visible, check the macOS menu bar near the system status items.
  • If launch at login does not work, open Espresso Pro settings and toggle launch at login off and on again.
  • If notifications do not appear, check macOS System Settings > Notifications > Espresso Pro.
  • If your Mac still sleeps after locking the screen, this is expected. macOS may apply stricter policies after lock, logout, or shutdown.
