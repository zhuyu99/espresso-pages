Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

Data Collection

Espresso Pro does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

The app does not require an account and does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, crash reporting, or telemetry SDKs.

Local Preferences

Espresso Pro stores app settings locally on your Mac using system preferences. These settings may include preferences such as default duration, launch-at-login choice, notification preference, and behavior toggles. This information remains on your device.

Network Access

Espresso Pro does not make network requests from app code.

Links in the About view may open your web browser to Espresso Pro's website, support page, privacy policy, or license page. Opening those links leaves the app and is handled by your browser.

Notifications

If enabled by the user, Espresso Pro may request macOS notification permission to send a local notification when a countdown ends. These notifications are created locally on the device.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on GitHub:

https://github.com/zhuyu99/espresso-pages/issues

Please do not include private account, payment, or device information in public issues.
