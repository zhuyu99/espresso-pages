The problem

Your Mac sleeps at exactly the wrong moment.

System Settings are too broad for temporary work. Terminal commands are powerful but easy to forget. Espresso gives you a visible, reversible control for one focused job: keep the Mac awake only when you choose.

How it works

Start a wake session from the menu bar.

Click Espresso, choose a duration, and keep working. Use a simple 30-minute session for free, or unlock custom and indefinite sessions when you need deeper control.

  • Toggle awake mode directly from the menu bar.
  • Use fixed, custom, or indefinite sessions with Lifetime Unlock.
  • Show remaining time in the menu bar when you want a visible countdown.
  • Let macOS return to normal sleep behavior when the timer ends.

FAQ

Common questions before downloading.

Will Espresso keep my Mac awake forever?

Only while you choose. You can use a timed session, turn it off manually, or let macOS return to normal sleep behavior when the timer ends.

Does it replace System Settings?

No. Espresso is intentionally focused on temporary wake sessions. It is not a full power-management suite.

Does it work without an account?

Yes. Espresso has no account system and stores preferences locally on your Mac.
